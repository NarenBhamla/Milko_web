About Us

Milko , Milko Biz is a smart daily milk delivery app designed to simplify life for both families and milkmen. Our mission is to ensure that every household receives fresh, hygienic milk every morning, hassle-free.

We connect customers and milk suppliers through a reliable digital platform, making milk management easier than ever. With Milko, you can schedule deliveries, track orders, manage subscriptions, and enjoy transparent billing.

For milkmen, Milko Biz provides a digital record-keeping system that helps them track deliveries, automate billing, and manage customers efficiently. This reduces manual errors and allows milkmen to focus on providing quality service.

Find Nearby Milkmen: Customers can easily find nearby milkmen using Milko, making it simple to get fresh milk even if they move to a new location. This feature ensures that everyone has quick access to reliable milk suppliers wherever they are.

Our Vision

To build a trustworthy digital bridge between milk suppliers and families across India, ensuring reliability, convenience, and transparency.

Our Mission

  • Deliver fresh, high-quality milk at your doorstep every morning
  • Make milk management simple and efficient for both customers and milkmen
  • Provide transparent billing and timely notifications
  • Support milk suppliers with digital tools to grow their business
  • Help customers easily find nearby milkmen, even in new locations
